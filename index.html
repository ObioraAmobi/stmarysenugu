About Us

St. Mary’s Hospital and Maternity is a multi-specialist boutique hospital located in a serene environment in GRA Enugu. As one of the oldest private hospitals in Enugu, we have devoted over four decades to caring for our patients. Our focus today is on delivering patient centered care in an efficient, qualitative manner. We provide conscientious assessment, intervention and treatment for numerous common and specialist conditions. Our patients are guaranteed customised and individualised healthcare to suit their needs. By working closely across all of our medical specialties, we endeavour to ensure that patients receive the most comprehensive care and treatment possible.

History

Dr. Benedict Vincent Obiora Amobi, an accomplished Consultant Gynaecologist and Igwe III of Ogidi, Anambra State founded St. Mary’s Hospital in 1972. Prior to establishing the hospital, Dr. Amobi graduated from University College Dublin, Ireland with a Medical Degree. Following the completion of his undergraduate degree, Dr. Amobi returned to Nigeria where he worked at Massey Street Hospital, Lagos before heading the General Hospital in Degema. In 1958, Dr. Amobi returned to Dublin to specialise in Obstetrics and Gynaecology. Upon his return, Dr. Amobi served in various capacities all over Eastern Nigeria before making the bold decision to establish St. Mary’s Hospital. Dr. Amobi was passionate about serving his people and strived to give back by meeting the needs of his community through clinical excellence. The principles of Dr. Amobi continue to shape the vision, mission and values of the hospital today. The current Managing Director of St. Mary’s Hospital is Dr. Sheila Enuoma Amobi.

Other Services

In addition to our dedicated in-house doctors and nurses, we have access to Specialists in most fields of Medicine.

To support our clinical team in achieving accurate diagnoses, we have a well-equipped Laboratory, an X-ray machine and a 4D Ultrasound scan. Our 24-hour in-house pharmacy facilitates prompt and timely commencement of treatments.

Our inpatient facilities include two suites, private and semi-private rooms, a general ward (male and female), a maternity ward with its own delivery suite, a paediatric ward and a new-born intensive care unit equipped with a neonatal incubator, radiant warmer and phototherapy units. In addition, there is a fully equipped operating theatre and recovery room and a smaller theatre for minor procedures.

At St Mary’s, you and your family are assured the best care wrapped in comfort.
